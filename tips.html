The "Devil's Advocate" Strategy

Use this to stress-test your business plans or strategic decisions before finalizing them.

Act as a senior strategic consultant known for rigorous risk assessment. I am going to present a business decision to [Insert Context]. Your goal is to critique this plan, identifying 3 potential failure points and 2 overlooked market risks. Be direct and constructive.

Best for: Planning
Coding

Explain Code to a Junior Dev

Great for documentation or understanding legacy codebases quickly.

Explain the following code snippet as if you are a senior engineer teaching a junior developer. Focus on the logic flow, potential edge cases, and why specific functions were used. Finally, suggest one optimization for readability. Code: [Insert Code]

Best for: Documentation

Meeting Minutes to Action Plan

Turn messy notes into a structured table of deliverables.

I am pasting rough notes from a meeting below. Please restructure this into a clean Markdown table with columns: 'Agenda Item', 'Decision Made', 'Action Item', 'Owner', and 'Deadline'. If a deadline wasn't mentioned, mark it TBD. Notes: [Insert Notes]

Best for: Admin

Tone & Style Shifter

Rewrite content for specific audiences without losing the core message.

Rewrite the following text to be more persuasive and executive-friendly. Remove jargon, focus on ROI, and keep the tone confident but professional. Aim for a Flesch-Kincaid grade level of 8. Text: [Insert Text]

Best for: Comms

Gap Analysis

Identify what is missing between your current state and desired future state.

Perform a Gap Analysis for [Company/Project].
Current State: [Insert State]
Desired Future State: [Insert Goal]
Identify the operational, technological, and skills gaps that bridge these two states. Present as a bulleted list prioritized by impact.

Best for: Analysis

The 80/20 Summarizer

Get the 20% of information that gives you 80% of the value from long docs.

Summarize the attached document using the Pareto Principle (80/20 rule). Identify the 20% of the insights that provide 80% of the value. Format the output as 'Key Insight' followed by 'Actionable Takeaway'. Document: [Insert Text]

Best for: Reading
