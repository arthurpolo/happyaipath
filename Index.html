About Jim Perry

A Leader at the Intersection of Technology, Education, and Wellness

Jim Perry is a seasoned data and technology leader with over 20 years of experience driving business transformation for Fortune 100 companies. His expertise spans AI, data strategy, and technology implementation, helping organizations become data-driven and customer-centric.

As an Adjunct Lecturer at The Ohio State University Fisher College of Business, Jim designs and teaches courses in AI Prompt Engineering and Data Mining, empowering the next generation of leaders to harness the power of AI. His commitment to education is matched by his dedication to well-being. As a certified yoga instructor, he helps individuals cultivate mindfulness and balance, principles he integrates into his leadership coaching.

This unique blend of deep technical knowledge, educational passion, and a focus on human-centered growth allows Jim to offer a holistic approach to AI adoption and leadership development.
