# SEO Audit & Recommendations (May 7, 2026)

## What was checked
- Meta title/description and robots directives
- Canonical URL coverage
- Sitemap and robots alignment
- Structured data coverage
- Internal link integrity
- Accessibility/semantic signals that impact SEO

## Immediate fix applied
- Fixed broken internal links in `blog-post-template.html` by replacing invalid parent-directory paths (`../...`) and placeholder related-post links with valid in-site targets.

## High-priority recommendations
1. Add **Organization + WebSite schema** sitewide and add **service-specific schema** (`Service`) on `services.html`.
2. Ensure every page has a **unique title** and **unique meta description** aligned to page intent.
3. Add `rel="canonical"` on all pages and verify no accidental canonical duplication.
4. Add `BreadcrumbList` schema to key interior pages (`services`, `about`, `blog`, `guide`, `tips`).
5. Create dedicated long-form pages targeting high-intent terms (e.g., "executive AI coaching", "AI team training workshop").

## Medium-priority recommendations
1. Improve image SEO:
   - Convert large JPG/PNG assets to modern formats where feasible.
   - Add explicit `width`/`height` on images to reduce CLS.
   - Ensure descriptive alt text for non-decorative images.
2. Strengthen internal linking with contextual links between service, guide, quiz, and blog posts.
3. Expand `llms.txt` with concise summaries and high-value URLs for AI crawlers.
4. Add FAQ sections to core commercial pages and mark up with `FAQPage` where appropriate.
5. Add author/reviewer blocks on blog posts to reinforce E-E-A-T.

## Technical QA recommendations
1. Run Lighthouse regularly and target:
   - Performance >= 90
   - SEO >= 95
   - Accessibility >= 95
2. Validate structured data with Google Rich Results Test after each major content update.
3. Keep sitemap `lastmod` accurate whenever pages change.
4. Track crawl/index status in Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster Tools.

## Content roadmap (next 60 days)
- Publish 4–6 intent-focused blog posts answering executive AI adoption questions.
- Add 1 case-study style page showing outcomes from coaching/training.
- Build 2 comparison pages (e.g., "AI coaching vs AI consulting").
